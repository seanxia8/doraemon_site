## Adjusted Rand Index

Adjusted Rand Index measures agreement between two partitions while correcting
for chance.

In compact form, $ARI$ compares an observed rand index against its expected
value under chance:

$$
ARI = \frac{RI - \mathbb{E}[RI]}{\max(RI) - \mathbb{E}[RI]}
$$

## Panoptic Quality

Panoptic quality combines recognition quality and segmentation quality for
instance-aware segmentation tasks.

## Masked-hit Recovery

Masked-hit recovery measures whether a method reconstructs intentionally hidden
detector activity from the visible event context.

## Linear Probe Suite

Linear probe suite scores evaluate whether a frozen representation supports
held-out detector tasks through lightweight supervised probes.
